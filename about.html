About Us

The Showcase of Innovation

TechCon is the premiere technological convention of the year. The brilliant minds behind industry leading companies join together at TechCon for a demonstration of human innovation at the highest standard. TechCon is an event where you can experience the future.
TechCon 2023 will be held in Silicon Valley, San Francisco, United States of America over the course of three days from December 15th - December 17th, 2023.
The headline speakers for TechCon 2023 are Elon Musk, Mark Zuckerberg, and Kanye West. The themes for Day 1, Day 2, and Day 3 of TechCon 2023 are Space Exploration, Gadgets & Devices, and Software, respectively.
